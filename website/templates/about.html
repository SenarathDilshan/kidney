ABOUT

Cancer Detection using Machine Learning & Image Processing

Kidney tumors are becoming increasingly prevalent in Sri Lanka. Over the past 20 years, the incidence of kidney tumors has doubled, accompanied by a corresponding rise in kidney tumor-related deaths. In Sri Lanka, kidney tumors have emerged as a significant cause of hospital mortality. One of the major issues identified is the prolonged time required to detect kidney tumors. This delay often allows the tumor to spread throughout the body. Many people in the general populace are unaware of the signs of kidney tumors, leading to a longer time for patients to recognize symptoms. Treatment for kidney tumors also comes with its own set of adverse effects. It's crucial to closely monitor the patient's health to prevent avoidable side effects. Therefore, a solution to this problem should include reducing time waste, providing a method for patients to recognize symptoms accurately, ensuring highly accurate kidney tumor detection, and implementing a better monitoring system. The proposed system aims to enable and guide patients in recognizing kidney tumor symptoms on their own, directing them to appropriate healthcare specialists, accurately detecting kidney tumors in their early stages, and monitoring the patient throughout treatment.
